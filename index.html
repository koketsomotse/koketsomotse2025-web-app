About Me

Founder of Billionaire Developers

Tech Leader | Senior Python/Django & React Engineer | International Speaker | STEM Advocate

Hi, I'm Koketso "KapFresh" Motseothata,based in the west of Johannesburg, South Africa also known as 'The Coolest Kid in STEM' , the young code advocate holds a number of tech skills such as Software Engineer, Full-Stack Web Developer, Game Developer, eSports and Gamming Enthusiast as well as Drone Pilot with Data Science and Machine Learning qualities.

Koketso Motseothata is a seasoned software engineer with over seven years of experience in Python, Django, React, and full-stack development. Based in Roodepoort, South Africa, Koketso is passionate about technology advocacy, education, and leadership, with expertise spanning AI, data science, robotics, and drone engineering.

Holding a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Software Engineering, Koketso is certified in AWS, Microsoft Azure, and various data science and machine learning disciplines. He has worked with top companies like Google, Microsoft, AWS, and Binance.

As a leader in the tech community, Koketso has served as a Google Developer Group Lead, AWS Community Builder, and Global AI Community Leader. He has mentored for Google Africa Developer Scholarship and Facebook's BuildForSDG, earning recognition as Mentor of the Year in 2020.

An international speaker at events like Gitex and PyCon Africa, Koketso also runs his startup, Billionaire Developers, where he creates tech content, runs workshops, and offers consulting. His mission is to foster technology literacy, inclusion, and innovation in the Fourth Industrial Revolution.
